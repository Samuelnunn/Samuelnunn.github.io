Full-Stack Projects:

Wing

Who knows you better than yourself? Your loved ones of course! Wing is fun and fresh take on modern dating. Have a friend set up a profile for you and let them match/message other users, and setting you up on dates on your behalf!

Petstagram

Petstagram is an a fully functional instagram clone. It allows users to upload pictures of their favorite pets, while allowing other users to gush over the uploaded pictures with commenting and likes.

What to Watch

What to Watch is a Goodreads clone with a focus on movies. Users can build a ever-expanding list of movies that have watched and rate those movies. They can then begin to filter and track movies that they wish to watch in the future. What to Watch allows fors a centralized system so that the user can remember which films they want to watch in the future.
